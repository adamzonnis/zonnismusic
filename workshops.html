Self Management for Independent Musicians

In this course you’ll learn:
● How to find venues and book shows
● How to apply for festivals
● The importance of social media and connecting with fans
● How to identify your musical goals and attain them
● Merchandise and how to get it
● Ways to make money with your music
● General information about how the music industry works
